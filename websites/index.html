Websnowman

What is Websnowman

Internet filtering and parental control software program, gives you the ability to control and monitor your children use of the Internet, irrespective of where you are: in the neighboring room, at work, or even on vacation, thus enabling you to protect your child from the dangers of the Internet.

Main Features

Block Websites
There are a number of pron websites on the Internet or some one you don't want to your children access to. The feacture "Website black list" will help you make it. Any website in the list won't be available when Websnowman runs in children mode.
Block search word
Now, the search engine so strong and easy-to-use, even the children can get the information they interest by keyword search conveniently. But a lot of pron information exists on internet. The WebSnowman provide a very useful feacture which can prevent children from these pron information. What you need to do is just specifiy some keywords you don't want your children to search. Then your children woun't get any information from the most popular search engin "Google" and "Yahoo".
Website Log
This feacture can record the websites your children visited, the words they used to search. You can know what your children do on the Internet.
Eyesight Protection
It's not good for children that sitting on the front of the computer for long time. So lock the computer after some time may be helpful for your children's eyesight.
Screen Snapshot
Sometimes, you want to know what your children do when you are away from them. The feature "Screen Snapshot" can record the content of computer screen intp pictures. You can review them when you have time. Moreover, Websnowman provides auot-clean feature. From time to time, it will clean up all there log files automatically. You needn't worry about the diskspace.
Online time control
Sometimes, you don't want your child to use Internet. This feature will help you make it. Just specify the time you want to block the Internet, all application cannot access to internet during the time.
Application Control
This feacture allows you to forbit your children using some applications. For example, you don't want to your children to play netgames or chat on line. What you need to do is just just add the relative application's path into the forbitten list. The application woun't be launched unless you switch to parent mode or remove it from the list.
