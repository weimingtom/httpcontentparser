Key Features

Website Blacklisted & Whitelisted

“Website Blacklisted” will block web sites with unwanted content that you prevent your children access to. You could also list your trustable Web sites with “Website Whitelisted”. With the option 'Just Enable white websites', your children can only browser those websites whitelisted.

More

Blacklisted Query

For now, the search engine is very powerful and easy to use. Even a child can get anything it interested by keyword search from Internet. Therefore, you will worry about the search engine may be misapplied by your children. The feature enables you to block some keywords you didn't want to your children to search by.

More

Screen Snapshots

You might often wonder what your children browser in Internet? “Screen Snapshots” give you the answer! “Screen Snapshots” can capture the whole screen, and save it into a JPEG file. You can specify the screen capture interval, and Family007 will capture the screen each interval automatically. So you can review these snapshots with a Family007 viewer.

More

Program Blacklisted

So many programs nowadays in computer! You want to prevent your children access some programs, such as games. You can add those programs into the “Program Blacklisted”. So the programs blacklisted cannot be launched by your children again.

More

Eye Protector

Children tend to use computer too excessively, which is really harmful, especially to eyes of Children. With “Eye Protector”, the computer screen will be locked at a given time interval that you specify. “Eye Protector” will remind your Children to do some physical exercises for a relaxation before sitting in front of computer again.

More

Time Controller

You might not want your children to access Internet at some specific time slots, for example, late middle-night. With “Time Controller”, you can specify the time slots that you want to stop the Internet. Then, all programs cannot access to Internet during the time slots you specified.

More

Webhistory record

“Web History Recorder” can track all visited websites and searching keywords. So you could monitor what exactly your children did at Internet.
